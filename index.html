The Internet Divide in the Rockaways

“Two-Rockaways” is a phrase commonly used to describe segregation within the peninsula. Through communities, businesses and more the Rockaways are ensuring who gets the best of treatment and the worst. Unfortunately, due to COVID-19 students have been forced to learn virtually which has exacerbated the digital divide plaguing Black and brown communities on the peninsula.
